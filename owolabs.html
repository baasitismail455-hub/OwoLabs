1 Introduction

This report provides a statistical analysis of serum calcium, albumin, and phosphate concentrations in malaria-infected and non-infected children. The analysis follows three specific objectives:

  1. Assess the serum levels of calcium, albumin, and phosphate in malaria-infected and non-infected children.
  2. Compare biochemical parameters across malaria and control groups.
  3. Determine correlations among parameters within malaria-infected children.

All analyses were performed in R, and the steps are detailed below.

  grepl('CONTROL', owo$sampleId, ignore.case = T) ~ 'control', #for control
  grepl('SAMPLE', owo$sampleId, ignore.case = T) ~ 'malaria',  #for malaria 
  TRUE ~ NA_character_
)

# remove std and na 
owo <- owo |> 
  filter(!is.na(status))
